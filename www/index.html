Blokus

Goal of this game is to have the most points when no player can place any piece anymore.
Each piece is worth as many points as it covers fields.
Each new piece has to be placed corner to corner to own pieces but must not touch own pieces field to field.

The fist piece for player one has to be placed onto (5,5) and the diagonal opposite for player two.

This site was created for the seminar "Analysis and implementation of mathematical games" by Prof. Dr. Kreuzer at the University Passau.
